Our Story

2019

How We Met

Our love story began in the most unexpected way. We first crossed paths at a cozy coffee shop in downtown, where Oleksandr was working on his laptop and Cathrine was reading her favorite book. A simple conversation about literature turned into hours of talking, and we both knew there was something special about that moment.

2021

Our First Adventure

Two years later, we took our first big trip together to the beautiful mountains of Montana. It was during this adventure that we discovered our shared love for nature, hiking, and quiet moments watching the sunset. We knew we wanted to explore the world together, hand in hand.

2024

The Proposal

On a magical evening in December, Oleksandr surprised Cathrine with a proposal at the very place where we had our first adventure in Montana. Surrounded by snow-covered peaks and twinkling stars, he got down on one knee and asked the question that changed our lives forever. Of course, she said yes!

2026

Our Wedding Day

Now, we're excited to celebrate our love with our closest family and friends in the breathtaking landscape of Montana. We can't wait to start this new chapter of our lives together and create many more beautiful memories as husband and wife.
