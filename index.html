What is a Vyshyvanka?

A vyshyvanka (–≤–∏—à–∏–≤–∞–Ω–∫–∞) is a traditional Ukrainian embroidered shirt or blouse. Each region has unique patterns and colors, often passed down through generations. The embroidery holds cultural significance and tells a story.

About the Tradition

  • Hand-embroidered with intricate geometric or floral patterns
  • Colors and designs vary by region
  • Often made from linen or cotton
  • Can be casual or formal depending on style
  • Worn with pride during celebrations
